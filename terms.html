Cryptography
It is a technique of secure communication for transfering or storing messages or data based on Mathematical and Computer Science Theory.
Proof of Work
It is the requirement to define an extensive computer calculation which basically protets blockchains from External cyber attacks
Byzantine Fault Tolerance
It is the dependability of a fault-tolerant computer system, particularly in distributed computing systems, where components may fail and there is imperfect information on whether a component has failed.
Double Spending
It is a fraudlent technique of spending same amount of money twice by copying or falsifyig.
Minning
A process using high computational power to validate the transactions by solving complex cryptographic puzzle.
Hash
A product of cryptographic work which connects the new block with its predecessor in the blockchain.
SHA-256
It is a cryptographic hash function with digest length of 256 bits.
Scrypt
It is a hash function alternative to SHA-256 and faster than that, used first in Litecoin.
Cryptonight
It is proof-of-work algorithm. It is designed to suitable for ordinary PCs.
